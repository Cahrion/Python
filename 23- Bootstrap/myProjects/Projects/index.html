Cycling For Help

Helping is an essential part of life. So, let me introduce you to the “Cycling For Help” project. We believe that this project, which is still in the design phase, will make sure people don’t see it as a difficult action. If you are having a bad day, go, and cycle it. We will use your cycling energy. Energy will transform into money as a solar panel does. In this way, your cycling energy helps us to donate a needed person. Your energy can help people and can change the world. Just one bicycle… We can install this bicycle in gyms, streets, or malls. It should be easy to reach. We want that, after 10 minutes of cycling, talk with yourself, and say “I burned approximately 100 calories, and it makes me help a needed person.”. We will develop a software and you can install it on your phone. And when you want to cycle, just match your phone with the bicycle. Owing to this app, you can see how many minutes did you cycle, how many calories did you burn. Just live healthily and let it make something good in the world. You can hit two birds with one stone: living healthily and donating to a needed person.
